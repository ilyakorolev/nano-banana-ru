## Tips for Better Prompts

1. **Be specific** — "blue" → "#3b82f6"
2. **Specify dimensions** — "16:9", "1080x1080", "24px grid"
3. **Describe mood** — "premium", "friendly", "corporate"
4. **Mention references** — "McKinsey style", "Apple aesthetic"
5. **Add technical details** — "sharp focus", "high contrast"
